My Hobby

Apps may be different, but skill is what really makes the magic happen 💁‍♀️✂️

Adobe After Effects

★★★★★

Insane for motion graphics and cool animations ✨ not the easiest to learn,

but once you get the hang of it. You can make your videos pop like crazy!

I mostly use it for transitions and title sequences

it’s giving cinematic vibes. 🎥💥

Adobe Premiere Pro

★★★★☆

My go-to for serious editing sessions 💻✂️

Great for cutting, layering audio, adding effects basically the full package for long-form vids.

A bit heavy on older laptops, but once it’s running smooth, it’s powerful !

Best when I want that “YouTube-pro-editor” look! 📺🎧

CapCut

★★★★★

Honestly? A lifesaver for quick mobile edits.

The auto captions, templates, and trendy filters sooo easy to use!

Perfect when I’m editing on the go or just need something TikTok-ready.

Minimal effort, max aesthetic ✂️📲

Canva

★★★★★

Super beginner-friendly for short-form video editing. 

Transitions and text effects are smooth, but sometimes a bit laggy on older phones. 

Still a fave for quick, clean edits! my go-to when I’m rushing! 💻💨

Adobe Photoshop

★★★☆☆

The OG for all things visual!

Whether I’m retouching selfies or designing thumbnails, this one’s a must.

Bit overwhelming at first, but so worth it once you know the tools.

Definitely my go-to for graphics, text layers, and ✨glow-ups✨
